Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DocScanner Pro ("we", "our", or "the App"), developed by Aditya Shrivastava, handles information when you use our document scanning and PDF creation application on Android.

By using DocScanner Pro, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

Effective date: April 19, 2025  |  Last updated: April 19, 2025

🔐

Permissions & Data We Access

DocScanner Pro requests the following Android permissions. All data stays on your device — we do not collect, upload, or sell it.

Permission Why it's needed
CAMERA To capture photos of documents for scanning. Camera frames are processed locally and never transmitted.
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE To let you import existing images or PDFs from your device storage.
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE To save scanned PDFs to your device (Android 9 and below only).
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES To access photos on Android 13+ for document import.
INTERNET Required by Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements.
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Checks connectivity status before loading ads.
🔒 No cloud uploads. All scanned documents and PDFs are stored exclusively on your device. We never access, transmit, or store your documents on external servers.
📢

Advertising (Google AdMob)

DocScanner Pro uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data to deliver interest-based ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage data and interaction events

This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

🚫

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information
  • We do not upload your documents, images, or PDFs to any server
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except AdMob, as described above)
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or crash reporting tools
👶

Children's Privacy

DocScanner Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

🛡️

Security

Since all document data is processed and stored locally on your device, your files are protected by your device's built-in security (screen lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device's OS updated for the best security protection.
